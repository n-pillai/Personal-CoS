## FAQ

**Do I really need Claude Pro?**

Yes. The Projects feature (which gives your CoS persistent memory and context) requires Claude Pro. There's no workaround.

**Can I use ChatGPT or another AI instead?**

You could adapt this, but it's specifically designed for Claude Projects. The structure and features may not translate directly.

**How long until I see results?**

Most people feel clarity within Week 1. Measurable progress on goals typically shows by Week 4. Real transformation happens around Month 3.

**What if I skip check-ins?**

The system only works with consistent use. Skip check-ins for a week = you've paused the system.

**Can I share my CoS with others?**

Your CoS is personalized to YOU. Others should build their own using this course.

**Is this just talking to Claude?**

Yes, but with critical differences:
- Persistent memory of your goals and context
- Programmed to act as YOUR strategic partner
- References your personal documents automatically
- Conversation history builds deep understanding over time

It's the difference between talking to a stranger vs. someone who knows you deeply.
