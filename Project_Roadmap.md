## WHAT COULD GO WRONG

### Technical Failures
- API costs spiral out of control
- Data loss bugs alienate users
- Performance issues at scale
- Security breach

**Mitigation:** Good architecture from start, monitoring, testing

### Product Failures
- No one wants this
- High churn, low retention
- Feature bloat ruins simplicity
- Competition beats you

**Mitigation:** MVP validation first, user feedback, focus

### Execution Failures
- Burnout from overwork
- Day job conflicts
- Can't maintain quality
- Scope creep kills momentum

**Mitigation:** Sustainable pace, clear boundaries, ruthless prioritization

### Market Failures
- Too niche
- Can't monetize
- No growth channels
- Regulatory issues

**Mitigation:** Market research, flexible business model, compliance
